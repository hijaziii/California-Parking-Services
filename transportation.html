Transportation

A unique quality that California Parking Services brings with it
to prestigious, high volume venues is its know-how in
setting up and managing complementary services to valet
parking. Over the years we have managed limousine, taxi,
shuttle, full car wash services and auto detail centers.
From experience, we have found that patrons of those
venues appreciate and utilize these services.
CPS offers the following transportation services at these venues:
- Casinos
- Hotels
- Medical Facilities
- Shopping Centers
